Key Dates

  • 1968: Open Era started
  • 1972: Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) established
  • 1973: First ATP ranking issued
  • 1990: ATP Tour started. Ranking points introduced

Player performance calculation

For each player at every period of time we calculate his performance over past calendar year including:

  • Title Score: Grand Slams (100 pts), Masters (50 pts), ATP 500 (25 pts)
  • Win Rate Bonus: (win_rate - 50%) × 100, max 50 pts
  • Top 10 Consistency: 0.6 pts per week in top 10, max 30 pts
  • Current Ranking: Top 5 (20 pts), Top 10 (15 pts), Top 20 (10 pts)
